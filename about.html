I’m Milan Čubić, a freelance user interface designer. Back in 2010. I cofounded Impaddo, a software company focused on development of applications for mobile, particularly Apple iOS platform. Shortly after, I moved on in search of fresh challenges, sold my company and started a new and exciting phase, as a freelancer.

Prior to cofounding a company I was employed as a designer and project manager where I designed iOS user interface solutions and managed projects for clients such as Swiss Post and Blick.ch.

Even before that, I was freelancing, designing iOS applications such as RTL TV and created iPhone app for viewing showtimes in local cinemas called Movies.

Photos on this site were taken by Nikola and Marina with Canon 5D Mark II Digital SLR and Nikon D3 respectively.
